About Me

Hello!!! My name is Sandy. I am married to the military and a working mother of 2 busy kids, a daughter who is 22 (still lives at home) and a son who is 14!!!! We are originally from Sherbrooke, Quebec. My husband was posted to Trenton, Ontario 3 1/2 years and we have been living here ever since. We love Ontario but miss home even though we make frequent visits.

Things I enjoy doing when alone are cooking, knitting and reading!!! I enjoy the peace and quiet whenever I can. As as family, we enjoy being outdoors during the summer. Boating, camping, and travelling are a few things we all enjoy doing together. During winter, other than travelling for hockey and spending time in the arenas, I become a hermit. I do not enjoy the cold.

I currently work for a medical cost containment company based out of Ottawa, Ontario. My position there is Director of IT Solutions. I love my job, the people I work with and the fact I get to work from home. I am hoping to grow my techinical knowledge and skills by taking the Bootcamp Coding course to further assist me in my current role.
